# TTMIK Grammar Map: Levels 1–10

Quick-reference mapping Korean grammar patterns to TTMIK levels and lessons.
Only core grammar patterns are listed.

| Lesson | Pattern | Core meaning |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| L4 | 이에요/예요 | is/am/are (copula) |
| L6 | 이/가 아니에요 | is not |
| L9 | 은/는, 이/가 | topic / subject marker |
| L10 | 있어요/없어요 | exist/have |
| L13 | V+고 싶어요 | want to do |
| L16 | V+아/어/여요 | present tense |
| L17 | V+았/었/였어요 | past tense |

## Notes
- **Why some lesson numbers are missing**: each TTMIK level has 30 lessons, but gaps are filled by
  Word Builder (汉字词 roots), Sentence Building Drills, Spacing/Contractions, and vocabulary lessons.
  Surface 汉字词 roots and contractions (e.g. 놓았 → 놨, 것은 → 건) in the vocabulary section rather
  than as grammar points.
- Lesson numbers follow the textbook/course platform order.
